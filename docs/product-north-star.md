# Product North Star

## 1-Sentence Product Goal
Lunara is a personal, album-first Plex player focused on reliable playback, digital gardening of the library, and context that meaningfully enriches listening.

## Prioritized User Outcomes
1. Reliable, trustworthy playback for albums and shuffle (online/offline behavior is clear and stable).
2. Digital gardening is easy: weed out albums, surface hidden gems, and keep key albums within easy reach.
3. Personal context (notes, themes, eras) adds meaningful depth without distracting from the music.
